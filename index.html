Utah

It also touches a corner of new Mexico in the southeast. The state is a center of transportation, education, information technology, and research, and major tourist destination for outdoor recreation. Salt Lake City, the state capital and largest city, is home to the headquarters of Jesus Christ Latter-day Saints (LDS Church).
